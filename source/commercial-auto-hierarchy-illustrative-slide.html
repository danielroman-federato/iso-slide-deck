RUNTIME ROOT
Commercial Auto Quote or Policy

Policy level

Policy-wide selections and filings that apply to the whole auto account.

CoveragesLiability, UM/UIM, PIP/no-fault, physical damage options, hired/non-owned.
FormsCoverage forms, endorsements, state notices, mandatory policy forms.
SchedulesFleet, symbol, and policy-level modifiers or credits.

State / garaging

Jurisdictional and territorial layer for rated vehicles.

StateFiling jurisdiction, law-driven coverages, financial responsibility context.
TerritoryGaraging location, operating territory, ZIP/county based keys.
Forms / RulesState-specific forms and exceptions selected by jurisdiction.

Vehicle level

The expected primary rated unit for commercial auto.

VehicleType, use, radius, size/weight, cost-new, model year, symbol, fleet flag.
CoveragesLiability, collision, comprehensive, specified causes, medical/no-fault.
SchedulesVehicle-level modifiers, credits, deductibles, limit factors.

Operator / exposure

Secondary detail that influences eligibility, rating, or referral.

DriversOperator data, age/experience, MVR and eligibility signals where supported.
OperationsBusiness use, commodities, radius, filings, leasing, hired autos.
Audit trailEvery factor and form decision should trace to Auto ERC content.
