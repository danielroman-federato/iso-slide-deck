Commercial Property Location

Location is the main container for buildings, occupancies, property values, and coverage schedules.

CoveragesLocation-level coverage selections and limits.
Forms / ScheduleLocation forms, building schedules, and rating modifiers.

Structure / Occupancy

Building and occupancy attributes drive many property rate keys.

StructureConstruction, protection, territory, sprinkler, stories, area, roof, year built.
OccupancyOccupancy class, mercantile/service/office attributes, exposure conditions.
CoveragesBuilding, ordinance or law, causes of loss, limits, deductibles, coinsurance.
Forms / ScheduleBuilding endorsements, protective safeguards, schedule credits/debits.

Property sub-lines

Page 66 shows Business Income, Personal Property, and Special Class as separate hierarchy branches.

Business IncomeIncome coverage, period of restoration, extra expense, limits, options.
Personal PropertyBPP values, stock, equipment, valuation, deductibles, causes of loss.
Special ClassSpecial property or exposure classes with their own coverages/forms.
Forms / ScheduleEach branch can carry its own coverages, forms, and schedule rows.
